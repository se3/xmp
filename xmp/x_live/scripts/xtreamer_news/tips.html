Native Language Keyword search
===============================

Here we are presenting tips for Native Language Keyword search for Xtreamer users.

Tips
===============================
(1) You can enter your Native Language Keywords from your Xtreamer UPnP Media Server to search in your tv screen. Its to make easy input of keywords which you want to search. You can enter several keywords at a time and save them to search.
To do so:
- Go to Setup->Live menu from webpage
- Click on "Make" button, a new window will appear.
- Enter your keywords here (seperated by new line!).
- Click "Save" button to save your keywords.
- Now whenever you need to search something while browsing from tv, you can press "Keyword" button of tv keybord screen.
- If you have multiple keywords, press "Keyword" key again and again to switch between them.
- When your desired keyword appars, press "OK" button to search for that keyword.
